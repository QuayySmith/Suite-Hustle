How To Play

Goal: build your hotel from Level 1 to Level 100. Check guests in, cook like a restaurant game, deliver using the elevator, clean rooms, hire staff, and upgrade the hotel graphics.

Orders: each guest gives a different room-service ticket. Staff can do separate orders automatically, but you still run the important tickets.

Elevator: it can break. Pay to repair it or deliveries stop.
