How To Play

Run your hotel from the inside. Check guests in, cook room-service orders, ride the elevator, deliver food, clean rooms, earn tips, and upgrade the whole hotel up to Level 100.

  • Cook orders before delivering them.
  • If the elevator breaks, pay repairs or lose time.
  • Hiring staff gives separate automatic orders.
  • Upgrade Hotel updates the look, assets, and graphics as your level rises.
